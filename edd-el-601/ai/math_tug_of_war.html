🐷 Tug of War 🦖

Take turns solving math questions! A correct answer pulls the flag towards your side. Incorrect answers push it away. First side to pull the flag home wins!

How to Play? 📖

  • 🎯 Two players take turns on the same device. Watch the top screen banner to see whose turn it is.
  • 🧠 Solve the math puzzle by tapping on the correct multiple choice button!
  • 💪 A correct answer pulls the central flag closer to your side by 1 step!
  • ⚠️ An incorrect answer allows the opposite side to pull the flag toward themselves!
  • 🏆 Pull the flag fully into your colored home zone to win the game!
