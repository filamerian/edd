GAME CONCEPT

A two-player (or player-vs-player, taking turns on the same device) tug-of-war game visualized as a horizontal rope with a flag/knot in the middle. On one end is Peppa Pig (pink color scheme), on the other end is George (light blue color scheme). Players take turns answering simple math problems. A correct answer pulls the flag closer to that player's side; an incorrect answer pushes the flag further away (toward the opponent's side). The game ends when the flag reaches either end of the rope, and that character is declared the winner.

1. Two players alternate turns (Player 1 = Peppa's side, Player 2 = George's side). Display whose turn it is clearly at the top of the screen.

2. Each turn, generate a simple math problem appropriate for elementary students:

5. On an incorrect answer:
