Course Description

Advanced Studies in Educational Planning and Management

The course introduces the student to the literature of educational planning to help in the acquisition of knowledge, skills, and tools required for the formulation of educational plans and strategies; the development of competence in conceptualizing the educational planning process; and the anticipation and determination of approaches and solutions to technical and practical problems of planning.
